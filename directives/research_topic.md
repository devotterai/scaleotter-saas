# Research Topic

## Goal
Conduct deep, autonomous web research on a specific topic and generate a comprehensive markdown report.

## Inputs
- `topic`: The subject to research (e.g., "The future of quantum computing", "Competitors to product X").

## Tools
- `execution/run_research.py`

## Outputs
- A markdown report saved to `.tmp/research_report.md`.
- Console logs of the research progress.

## Steps
1.  Run `python execution/run_research.py "<topic>"`.
2.  Verify the report exists at `.tmp/research_report.md`.
3.  Read and summarize the report if needed.
